## About these notes

This is a collection of co-reading notes for *Infinite Jest* by David Foster Wallace (Little, Brown and Company, 1996). Page references (*e.g.*, "pp. XXX") refer to the **first hardcover edition**. Footnotes are indicated by their number (e.g., "fn. XXX"), not their page number.

The notes were originally compiled as a shared co-reading project by [Rob Friesel (founddrama)](https://github.com/founddrama). This site is a fork of that [original repository](https://github.com/founddrama/infinite-notes), migrated from nanoc to Jekyll and extended with an interactive relationship graph.

## The book

*Infinite Jest* was first published in February 1996 by Little, Brown and Company. 1,079 pages including 388 endnotes. It is a very long book about addiction, entertainment, tennis, Quebecois separatism, and what it means to be a human being.
